# A good day

## Passage

vidivo veon in ta bazare. emerivi es pomeson. dicivo "salve",
ma ne audivi meon. poste, kumvadivos intra ta parko.
risivi, ete parlivos. sive e die bone.

## Translation

I saw you at the market. You bought apples. I said hello,
but you did not hear me. Later, we met at the park. You smiled,
and we talked. It was a good day.
