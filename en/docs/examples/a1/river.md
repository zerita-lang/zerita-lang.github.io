# A walk by the river

## Passage

save ta solis lumini.
pasave e vipueris ad ta fluve.
vidave e peskon magni.
saltave ta peskis.
ridave ta vipueris.
vadave ad ta dome laeti.

## Translation

The sun is bright.
A boy walks to the river.
He sees a big fish.
The fish jumps.
The boy laughs.
He goes home happy.
