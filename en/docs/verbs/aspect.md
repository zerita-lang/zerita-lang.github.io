# Aspect

In zerita there are 9 aspects, which are used to indicate the timing
of the action of the verb.
They are three triplets of them.
Each triplet contains a simple, a perfect, and a prospective aspect

| Progression/Perfectivity | Simple | Perfect | Prospective |
| ------------------------ | ------ | ------- | ----------- |
| Simple                   |        | -il     | -ol         |
| Progressive              | -um    | -ilum   | -olum       |
| Habitual                 | -em    | -ilem   | -olem       |

## Examples

- _potivo e akvon:_
    - I drank water
- _potivilo e akvon:_
    - I had drunk water
- _potivolo e akvon:_
    - I was about to drink water
- _potivumo e akvon:_
    - I was drinking water
- _potivilumo e akvon:_
    - I had been drinking water
- _potivolumo e akvon:_
    - I was about to start drinking water
- _potivemo e akvon:_
    - I had the habit of drinking water
- _potivilemo e akvon:_
    - I had had the habit of drinking water
- _potivolemo e akvon:_
    - I was about to start having the habit of drinking water
