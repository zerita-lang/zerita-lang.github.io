# Tense

## Veritas

Zerita has a timeless tense, the veritas tense.
The veritas tense is used to describe a timeless truth, a scientific fact, or an unchanging state,
anything that is true within a specific context, regardless of past, present, or future.
In the indicative mood, it's formed using the "-ev" suffix.

### Examples

1.  _glakeves akvatis in 0°C._

    - **translation:** Water freezes at 0°C.

1.  _edrevo postcenaton post cenate._

    - **translation:** I always eat dessert after dinner.

## Timed tenses

There are 3 timed tenses, and their suffixes are:

- **Present:** -av
- **Past:** -iv
- **Future:** -ov
