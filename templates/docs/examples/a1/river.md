# A walk by the river

## Passage

save ta solis lumine. pasave e vipueris ad ta fluve.
vidave e peskon magne. salte ta peskis. ride ta vipuer.
vadave ad dome laete.

## Translation

The sun is bright. A boy walks to the river. He sees a big fish.
The fish jumps. The boy laughs. He goes home happy.
