# <x-trans>Aspect</x-trans>

<x-trans>In zerita there are ${9} aspects, which are used to indicate the timing
of the action of the verb.</x-trans>
<x-trans>They are three triplets of them.</x-trans>
<x-trans>Each triplet contains a simple, a perfect, and a prospective aspect</x-trans>

| <x-trans>Progression</x-trans>/<x-trans>Perfectivity</x-trans> | <x-trans>Simple</x-trans> | <x-trans>Perfect</x-trans> | <x-trans>Prospective</x-trans> |
| -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| <x-trans>Simple</x-trans>                                      |                           | -il                        | -ol                            |
| <x-trans>Progressive</x-trans>                                 | -um                       | -ilum                      | -olum                          |
| <x-trans>Habitual</x-trans>                                    | -em                       | -ilem                      | -olem                          |

## <x-trans>Examples</x-trans>

- _potivo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I drank water</x-trans>
- _potivilo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I had drunk water</x-trans>
- _potivolo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I was about to drink water</x-trans>
- _potivumo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I was drinking water</x-trans>
- _potivilumo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I had been drinking water</x-trans>
- _potivolumo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I was about to start drinking water</x-trans>
- _potivemo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I had the habit of drinking water</x-trans>
- _potivilemo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I had had the habit of drinking water</x-trans>
- _potivolemo e akvon:_
    - <x-trans>I was about to start having the habit of drinking water</x-trans>
